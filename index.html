The Initiative

I wanna dedicate this page to discussing the merit around giving to shelters and the means by which it can be done. As I've always loved cats, I thought it would be best to tailor this initiative to them specifically. While it may seem as though shelters have this job covered, they are often overworked and without much support. Only with the community's help can such shelters truly support all the animals they hold.

Care Packages

One of the best ways I've heard to help shelters is through care package donations to help take some financial burden off of them. Things like food, kitty litter, bedding and toys can all bring some more quality to the lives of unfortunate cats. As packages are assembled with these commodities in mind, the community can help shelters keep up with all the entries they may have and not leave any animals behind.

Other Approaches

While care packages are one of the more simple ways of helping shelters, there are many other ways we can help cats in need. These ways are best discovered through researching and reaching out to local institutions, seeing what they may need help with. From volunteering to bring more help to adopting in taking responsibility for one of their animals, we can undeniably play a part in working with animal shelters to give cats better lives. If we don't, more helpless cats may be missed and left to fend for themselves.
