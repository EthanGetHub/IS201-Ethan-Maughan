Why I'm Passionate About Cats









Click to See the Cute Cat

What Got Me Started

When I was back home, I came across an abandoned litter of kittens. It looked like somebody had dumped them on the side of the road, so my family took them in for a bit. While we were able to try and care for them for a while, my dad's allergies eventually caught up to us and they needed to be taken somewhere else. We brought them to an animal shelter and that was the last I heard of them. I want life in those shelters to be as good as possible for cats like those.

Greater Motivation

Below is a graph demonstrating just how many cats and dogs that shelters around the US take in, and with all those entries, community help definitley wouldn't hurt.

What Gave Me the Idea of Care Packages

I wanted to think of ways I could help cats and, while researching, I came across this video and realized that there are significant ways I can help cats in shelters.

Why I Want to Help Cats in Shelters

  1. I've cared for cats who've gone into shelters

  2. Shelters are often overworked
    • There aren't too many dedicated cat shelters
    • The ones that do exist are overflowing

  3. There are ways I can leviate the workload from shelters
    • Making care baskets gives them resources they might've not otherwise had
    • Care baskets make cats more appealing to adopters and more cats can be adopted
